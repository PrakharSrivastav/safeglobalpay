Charges, Reserves and Payout obligations:
Set-up Fee: $ 200 (This is one time payment to use AyjoConsulting’s portal. This will help us give you access to our system and also provide you with a unique username and password to log in and start accepting payments)
  • Security Deposit - $500 (The security deposit is collected to set off any payments due from you to AyjoConsulting in case you are unable to meet your payment obligation. The security deposit will not accrue any interest with the passage of time, and shall be refunded after making appropriate deductions or in full 180 days after the termination of your contract with AyjoConsulting
  • Rolling Reserve - 10% of your corresponding month’s gross sales amount. The rolling reserve will be maintained for a period of 3 months.
  • Service Charge - AyjoConsulting will charge you a service providing charge of 25% of your gross sale proceeds per transaction.
  • Payout Period - Monthly. Payout from AyjoConsulting to you will be credited directly to your corporate bank account at the end of every month after deducting any dues that you owe AyjoConsulting, any of its third party affiliates, after adjusting for any refund/chargeback and the penalty therein.
  • Payout wire charges - Monthly payouts incur a flat fee of $50 per payout. You can request additional payouts in a month by paying an extra $25 over the flat fee of $50.
  • Chargeback Penalty - $30 over the actual chargeback amount. Chargebacks are detrimental to the goodwill of a business and we strongly refrain from doing business with partners/vendors whose accounts exhibit a series of chargebacks which AyjoConsulting feels are inappropriate to continue with. AyjoConsulting strongly encourages you to provide fair services for continued professional relations
  • Refund processing fee - 5% of the gross sales amount. If a customer requests a refund AyjoConsulting will process that refund and the entire amount plus an additional refund processing fee will be deducted from your debut balance with AyjoConsulting.
The pricing above applies to payments in US dollars. Any international transfer of funds will be at the exchange rate as specified by the bank at the time of making the transfer. All charges in respect of the transfer will be borne by the respective merchant/user of the services of AyjoConsulting.
